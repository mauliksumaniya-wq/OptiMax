How it Works

The science behind our AI Image Reconstruction

Bilinear Interpolation

Traditional tools just stretch pixels, creating "blocks." OptiMax uses AI to analyze existing data before adding new pixels.

02

Neural Networking

Our ESRGAN model predicts textures. If your photo has a blurry eye or skin, the AI knows what a sharp eye looks like and reconstructs it.
